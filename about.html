About TigerType

TigerType is a free, browser-based typing speed test and training platform built for people who want to improve their keyboard skills without distractions. No accounts. No installations. No clutter. Just you, the text, and your fingers.

The platform was created with a simple belief: the best typing tool is the one that gets out of your way. Most typing sites are crowded with animations, leaderboards, social features, and noise that pull focus away from the one thing that actually matters — deliberate, accurate practice.

TigerType strips all of that away. What remains is a clean, fast, focused environment designed to help you build real typing skill through consistent repetition and honest measurement.

What TigerType Offers

TigerType is built around three core tools, each designed to develop a different aspect of typing skill.

⏱️ Timed Typing Tests Choose 30, 60, or 120 seconds. Pick your difficulty. Get an instant WPM and accuracy score you can trust.
🎯 Practice Zone No countdown. No pressure. Type at your own pace with live feedback to build control before chasing speed.
📖 Beginner Lessons Three structured lessons covering home row position, accuracy fundamentals, and building a smooth typing rhythm.
🏆 Local Leaderboard Your top scores are saved on your device so you can track personal progress without creating an account.
📊 Rank System Earn ranks from Cub to Apex Tiger as your WPM improves. A visual progress bar shows how close you are to the next level.
📱 Works Everywhere Fully responsive and runs entirely in your browser. No app, no download, no signup required.

Dedicated Tests for Every Duration

Beyond the main typing test, TigerType offers four dedicated timed test pages, each built around a specific duration and training purpose.

Who TigerType Is Built For

TigerType is designed for anyone who types regularly and wants to do it better. That covers a wider range of people than most realize.

Students

Faster, more accurate typing means less time transcribing notes and more time thinking. Students preparing for timed exams, online coursework, or research writing benefit directly from structured typing practice.

Office Professionals

Email, reports, data entry, and documentation are the backbone of most office roles. Improving typing speed by even 10 to 15 WPM can save meaningful time across a full work week. Many employers also use typing tests as part of their hiring process — TigerType helps you prepare for those assessments.

Remote Workers

In remote work environments, written communication replaces most verbal interaction. Speed and accuracy in typing directly affect how quickly you can respond, collaborate, and produce output throughout the day.

Developers and Writers

People who spend hours writing code, documentation, or long-form content benefit from typing that keeps pace with their thinking. At higher WPM, the keyboard stops being a bottleneck and becomes invisible.

Beginners

If you are new to touch typing or have never been formally taught, TigerType's structured lessons cover the fundamentals — home row position, accuracy before speed, and building rhythm — in a way that is practical rather than theoretical.

The Design Philosophy

TigerType is intentionally minimal. The dark interface reduces eye strain during longer sessions. There are no pop-ups, no mandatory logins, no social comparisons, and no gamification designed to keep you on the site longer than you need to be.

The goal is to give you a focused environment, a reliable score, and enough structured content to improve — then get out of your way. Real skill development happens through deliberate repetition, not through engagement mechanics.

Every design decision on TigerType comes back to one question: does this help the user type better? If the answer is no, it does not belong on the page.
