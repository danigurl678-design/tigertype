Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

TigerType is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use TigerType, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

TigerType does not store the text you type during typing tests or practice sessions. All typing activity is processed locally in your browser to calculate words per minute (WPM), accuracy, and error counts. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Your best scores may be saved to your browser's local storage so your leaderboard persists between visits. This data stays on your device and is never shared.

Cookies and Advertising

TigerType uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to TigerType and other sites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, visit How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.

Analytics

TigerType may use analytics tools to understand general usage patterns such as page views, session duration, and device type. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

Third-party services including advertising and analytics providers operate under their own privacy policies. TigerType does not control how these services collect or use data. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

Children's Privacy

TigerType is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the site evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of TigerType after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, contact us at: tigertype.app@gmail.com
