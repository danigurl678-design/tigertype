About TigerType

TigerType is a free online typing test and training platform built to help students, professionals, and everyday users improve their words per minute and typing accuracy. The site offers timed typing tests, a pressure-free practice zone, structured beginner lessons, and a local leaderboard to track personal progress.

TigerType is designed to be clean, fast, and distraction-free — because the best typing tool is one that stays out of your way and lets you focus on the keys.

For full details on how TigerType works, visit the About section on the homepage.
